About This Prototype

This automated Railway Fitting Management Prototype integrates IoT-enabled QR codes, AI-driven analytics, and predictive maintenance to enhance railway safety and efficiency. Inspectors can scan fittings, view warranty and performance history, and AI predicts potential failures. The system supports role-based access: Admins manage everything, Workers log inspections, and Suppliers track their delivered fittings.

Future Scope: This prototype demonstrates how automation, real-time monitoring, and AI can revolutionize railway maintenance by reducing downtime, ensuring safety, and optimizing resource use.
